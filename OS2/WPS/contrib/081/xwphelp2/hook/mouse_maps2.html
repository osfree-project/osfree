The second "Mouse Mappings" page is inserted by &xwp; to allow for configuring allows you to configure more mouse features which are implemented by the &xwp; PM hook. Even more hook settings are on the "Movement" page.

All settings on this page take effect immediately.

Press "Undo" to restore the settings that were in use before you opened this notebook page.
