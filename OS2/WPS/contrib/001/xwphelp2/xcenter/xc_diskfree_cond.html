The &xcenter; "Diskfree" widgets allows you to monitor the free space on your disk drives.

The &xcenter; "Diskfree" widget in "condensed style", like the "Diskfree" widget in &warpcenter; style, allows you to view the free space on a single drive. However, this widget is most useful in showing several drives at once. Right-click onto the widget and select the drives you want to monitor from the "Drives" submenu.

You will also notice that the widget will "flash" a drive temporarily if it has detected that the free space on the drive has changed. This will even happen if the free space has only changed by a single byte (and will thus not change the free space display in the widget).

This might be useful to find out which disks are currently being written to in the background. However, a disk write can only be detected if the free space on the drive changes during the write operation; if, for example, an existing file is completely overwritten without changing its size, this will not be detected.

Note that you can drag and drop fonts and colors onto the widget. Besides, it is sizeable.
